Top service issues which will be of most prioirty to fix

Potholes

Potholes damage vehicles and pose risks to road users. Our teams will prioritize their repair using quality material to ensure long-lasting fixes and smoother roads.

Streetlights

Broken or non-functional streetlights contribute to safety concerns at night. Maintenance will be conducted regularly to restore proper lighting in all neighborhoods.

Water Leaks

Leaking water pipes waste resources and damage infrastructure. Repair crews will address leaks swiftly to conserve water and prevent further damage.

Electricity Outages

Power disruptions due to downed lines or offline transformers affect communities severely. Response teams are deployed to restore power quickly and safely while preventing further electrical hazards.
